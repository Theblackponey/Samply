FAQ

Before you buy.

Is this a subscription?

No. This is a one-time $49 licence for Sonus desktop access.

Where are my samples stored?

Your audio files stay on your machine and drives. Sonus stores account, licence, and machine status online, while the sample library stays local.

How many machines can I use?

Each account can register two machines. You can remove a machine from the dashboard to free a slot.

What happens after payment?

The website marks your account as premium, then the desktop app unlocks when you sign in with the same account.

Can I download before buying?

Yes. You can create an account and download the app before buying. Premium features unlock after purchase.

Do I need internet every time?

You need internet for account and licence checks. Your samples and analysis data remain local.
